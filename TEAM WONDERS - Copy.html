Team Topic

Transmogrification

Transmogrification is the act of changing into a different form of appearance. Another name of transmogrification is to transform, convert and metamorph. Example of transmogrification is when a fairy tale frog transforms into a prince.

HOW TRANSMOGRIFICATION RELATE TO NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT

National development is a way of improving a country in all aspect for the benefit of her citizen

A country can undergo transmogrification through the following process:

  1. Economic transformation a country economy can undergo transmogrification through industrialization, technological advancement.
  2. infrastructure development transmogrification could represent the transformation of Nigeria's infrastructure, such as upgrading transportation system.
